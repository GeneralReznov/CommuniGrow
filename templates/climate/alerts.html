Understanding Alert Levels

Low Risk

General weather advisories and seasonal reminders. Continue normal activities with awareness.

  • • Weather advisories
  • • Seasonal preparations
  • • General awareness
Moderate Risk

Weather conditions require attention and possible precautions. Monitor updates closely.

  • • Storm watches
  • • Heat advisories
  • • Flood potential
High Risk

Dangerous conditions requiring immediate action. Follow emergency procedures.

  • • Severe storm warnings
  • • Flood warnings
  • • Extreme heat/cold
